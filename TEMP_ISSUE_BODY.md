# OctoAcme Project Management Process Documentation

Welcome to the OctoAcme Project Management documentation suite. This collection of guides helps teams plan, execute, and continuously improve projects with clarity, collaboration, and measurable outcomes.

## Our Approach

OctoAcme follows a **five-phase lifecycle** for all cross-functional projects:

1. **Initiation** — Validate business need, align stakeholders, confirm success criteria
2. **Planning** — Break down work, estimate scope, identify dependencies, create timeline
3. **Execution & Tracking** — Build incrementally, track progress, manage blockers
4. **Release & Deployment** — Prepare for production, deploy safely, verify success
5. **Retrospective & Improvement** — Capture learnings, implement improvements, celebrate wins

Our principles guide all work:
- **Customer-first**: Prioritize customer value and usability
- **Iterative delivery**: Ship small, testable increments
- **Clear ownership**: Every project has named PM and Product Lead
- **Data-informed**: Measure impact and iterate based on evidence
- **Psychological safety**: Encourage feedback and learning

### Key Artifacts You'll Create

- **Project One-Pager**: Problem, goal, success metrics, stakeholders, timeline
- **Project Charter**: Approved scope, resources, milestones, risks
- **Backlog**: Prioritized work items with acceptance criteria and estimates
- **Risk Register**: Tracked risks with mitigation plans and owners
- **Release Notes**: Summary of changes, migration steps, known issues
- **Retrospective Notes**: Learnings and action items for continuous improvement

### Common Workflows

**Starting a New Project?**
1. Read [Project Management Overview](octoacme-project-management-overview.md) for context
2. Follow [Project Initiation Guide](octoacme-project-initiation.md)
3. Move to [Project Planning](octoacme-project-planning.md) once approved

**In Active Delivery?**
1. Reference [Execution & Tracking](octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) for daily standups and progress management
2. Consult [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md) when issues arise
3. Use team roles from [Roles and Personas](octoacme-roles-and-personas.md)

**Ready to Release?**
1. Follow the [Release & Deployment Guide](octoacme-release-and-deployment.md)
2. Prepare communication using [Risk Management & Communication](octoacme-risks-and-communication.md) templates

**After Project Close?**
1. Run a retrospective using [Retrospective & Continuous Improvement](octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md)
2. Implement action items in the next project cycle
