# OctoAcme Project Management Documentation

Welcome to the centralized hub for OctoAcme's project management processes. This collection of documents provides guidance for managing projects from initiation through closure, ensuring consistent, repeatable execution across all teams.

## Our Approach

OctoAcme follows a customer-first, iterative delivery model with clear ownership, data-informed decisions, and psychological safety. We believe in:
- Delivering small, testable increments
- Maintaining transparent communication across teams
- Learning and improving continuously
- Empowering teams with clear processes and ownership

## Project Lifecycle at a Glance

Our projects follow a five-phase lifecycle:

1. **Initiation** - Validate business need, align stakeholders, create project one-pager
2. **Planning** - Break work into shippable increments, identify dependencies and risks
3. **Execution** - Build, test, review, and iterate with daily standups and weekly syncs
4. **Release** - Deploy to production with safety checks and rollback plans
5. **Close & Retrospective** - Capture learnings and drive continuous improvement

## Communication Cadence

- **Daily** - Team standups (15 min)
- **Twice-weekly** - Delivery team standups
- **Weekly** - PM + Product Lead sync, Stakeholder status updates
- **At milestones** - Retrospectives and demos

## Key Principles

- **Customer-first** - Prioritize customer value and usability
- **Iterative delivery** - Deliver small, testable increments
- **Clear ownership** - Each project has a named PM and Product Lead
- **Data-informed** - Measure impact and iterate based on evidence
- **Psychological safety** - Encourage feedback and learning
